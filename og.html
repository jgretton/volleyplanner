Intermediate Serve Receive & Transition — 75 min

12 players Intermediate 75 min 6 drills
Warm-up 10 min

Dynamic Court Warm-Up

Players jog the court perimeter with dynamic movements. High knees, lateral shuffles, arm circles, and light passing to warm the shoulders.

Technical 15 min

Pepper Pairs — Platform Focus

Players pair up and pepper continuously. Focus on platform angle and a firm, steady base. Rotate pairs every 3 minutes.

Progressive 15 min

3-Passer Serve Receive

Three passers in a W formation receive live serves. Setter targets the OH and middle. Rotate passers every 5 serves.

Game-based 20 min

Serve Receive Wash Game

Competitive scrimmage focusing on sideout efficiency. Server wins the point with an ace or error; receiving team wins with a kill.
